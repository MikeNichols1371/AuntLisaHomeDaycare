About

Welcome to Aunt Lisa's Home Daycare. Established since 2004, I am here to provide all your needs as a daycare provider. I'm New York State certified in Adult and Pediatric First Aid, CPR, as well as AED trained. Each child will be provided with a healthy breakfast, as well as lunch, also including a wide array of activities to enhance individual play and learning, giving your child a fun, activity packed day, while also taking the time to do learning activities such as memory, spelling, math, and many others to get them school ready. If you are looking for a safe, well managed daycare for your child, look no further. If you are interested in enrolling your child, please reach out to me via email, phone, and or Facebook to schedule a time to visit.
